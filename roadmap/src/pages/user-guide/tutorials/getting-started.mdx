Welcome! This tutorial walks through your first few minutes on the platform. It's
plain Markdown — edit `src/pages/user-guide/tutorials/getting-started.mdx`.

## 1. Sign in

Use your organization SSO credentials. If sign-in fails, check the
[status page](/roadmap/status) for authentication incidents.

## 2. Open your workspace

Your workspace is where your team's features and data live. An administrator
assigns you to one during onboarding.

## 3. Check platform status

The [status page](/roadmap/status) shows live service health, scheduled
maintenance, and recent incidents. Check it whenever something looks off.

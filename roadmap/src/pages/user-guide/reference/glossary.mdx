Common terms, defined once.

## Workspace

The top-level container for a team's projects, members, and settings. Every user
belongs to at least one workspace.

## Project

A unit of work inside a workspace. Projects hold items and can be shared
independently.

## Incident

A recorded disruption to a service, shown on the status page with a severity and
timeline until it is resolved.

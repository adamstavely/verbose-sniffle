This guide assumes you already have a workspace and administrator access.

## Send an invite

Open **Workspace settings → Members** and choose **Invite**. Enter one or more
email addresses and pick a role.

## Choose a role

- **Member** — can view and edit shared content.
- **Admin** — can also manage members and workspace settings.

## Resend or revoke

Pending invites appear in the members list. Use the row menu to resend or revoke
an invite that hasn't been accepted.

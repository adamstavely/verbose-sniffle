You can subscribe to platform notifications without an account.

## Subscribe to incident emails

On the [status page](/roadmap/status), enter your email in the **Get incident
notifications** box. You'll receive updates for incidents and scheduled
maintenance.

## Stop receiving emails

Every notification email includes an unsubscribe link. Using it stops all further
messages to that address.

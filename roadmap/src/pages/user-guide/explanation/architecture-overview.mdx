This page is background reading — it explains *why* the platform is shaped the
way it is, rather than how to do a specific task.

## Workspaces as boundaries

A workspace is the unit of isolation. Access, billing, and data all sit inside a
workspace, which keeps one team's information cleanly separated from another's.

## Services behind the scenes

The features you use are backed by shared services — authentication, search,
storage, and messaging. The status page reports on each of these so you can tell
whether an issue is local to your workspace or platform-wide.

## Why status is honest about the unknown

When telemetry is unavailable, the status page shows an *unknown* state rather
than assuming everything is healthy. It's better to say "we're not sure" than to
imply a guarantee we can't back up.

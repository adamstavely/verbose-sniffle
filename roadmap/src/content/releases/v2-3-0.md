## Highlights

- **Dark mode** across the entire Help Center, with a toggle in the top navigation that remembers your choice.
- **Search** — press the magnifier in the nav or use the box on the home page to search every guide and release.
- **Tags** on documentation, with a new `/tags` browser to explore topics.

## Fixes

- Documentation prose is now legible in dark mode (headings, code, and body text follow the theme).
- Status badges use a single, consistent style with clearer color coding.
